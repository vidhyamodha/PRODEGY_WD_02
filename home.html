About Us

Welcome to our Stopwatch Web application! This application allows you to measure time intervals accurately using a digital stopwatch.

Instructions

  1. Press the "Start" button to begin timing.
  2. You can press the "Lap" button to record lap times while the stopwatch is running.
  3. To stop the stopwatch, press the "Stop" button.
  4. If you wish to reset the stopwatch and clear all lap times, press the "Reset" button.
